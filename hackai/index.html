What is Hack AI?

🤖

Agentic AI Workshop

A full-day hands-on workshop on Agentic AI from 9AM–5PM on Day 1. Learn, build, and ship real AI agents.

⚡

24-Hour Hackathon

Starting 5PM on Day 1, teams compete in a grueling 24-hour sprint to build the most impactful AI solutions.

🏆

Up to 300 Participants

Open to ~250 workshop attendees and up to 300 hackathon participants. Limited seats — register fast!

🎓

Certification & Benefits

All participants receive official certification, EduRev benefits, and CA benefits upon participation.

Event Structure

DAY 1 6th April 2026
09:00 AM – 05:00 PM

Agentic AI Hands-on Workshop

Deep dive into agentic AI frameworks, tools, and real-world applications. Hands-on sessions with industry tools.

05:00 PM onwards

🚀 Hackathon Begins!

The 24-hour clock starts. Teams assemble, problem statements revealed. Let the building begin!

DAY 2 7th April 2026
All Day

Hackathon Continuation

Keep building! Mentors available for guidance. Push your project to the finish line.

Afternoon

Final Submissions & Judging

Lock in your code, prepare your pitch. Judges evaluate projects on innovation, impact, and execution.

By 05:00 PM

🎉 Closing Ceremony

Winners announced, prizes distributed, certificates awarded. Event concludes.
