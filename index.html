FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before registering.

What is the registration fee and is selection guaranteed? +
The registration fee is ₹49 — a small commitment filter to ensure every seat is filled with a serious builder. Payment does NOT guarantee selection. If you are not selected, you receive a full refund. This keeps the quality high for everyone.
What are the team requirements? +
Teams must have a minimum of 1 member and a maximum of 4 members. At least 1 female member per team is preffered.
How many participants will be selected? +
Seats are strictly limited to 180 participants. Applications will be reviewed and the most promising teams will be selected. This is intentional — we want a curated, high-quality cohort, not a massive crowd.
What if I have CA exams during the event? +
We fully understand academic commitments. If you have CA exams during the event dates, you are allowed to leave the venue and return. Please inform the organizers in advance so we can accommodate your schedule.
What does the final project submission require? +
All final projects must be deployed on a cloud platform (AWS, GCP, Azure, Vercel, Render, etc.). A working live link is required for submission. Local-only demos will not be accepted. This requirement is intentional — it builds real deployment skills.
Who can participate? +
The hackathon is primarily for LPU students. Participants should have basic programming knowledge and interest in AI/ML. You don't need to be an AI expert — the Day 1 workshop is designed to bring everyone up to speed.
What perks are included during the event? +
All selected participants get: 1 night dinner, snacks throughout the event, fun gifts via mini-games, and a jamming session. Plus: direct mentor access, LinkedIn Masterclass, and guest speaker interaction — all included.
